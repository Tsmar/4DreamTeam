# Wiki Blueprint

Use this mode to design future wiki content without presenting unimplemented behavior as fact.

## Rules

1. Use `4dt-wiki` for page creation and updates.
2. Mark future or unverified content as `proposed`.
3. Use `4dt-sources` only for approved source-backed context.
4. Add task timeline evidence through `4dt-board` when the blueprint belongs to a task.
5. Validate with `4dt-wiki validate`.

Do not read or write wiki storage directly.
